Welcome to Arcade Game

Game Rules:

The player have to do five rounds from the land to the sea. In order to win the game, the player need to finish the rounds with an score equal or over 1000 points. Anytime the player hit an enemy, the score will be decremented 100 points, if the score became negative, the game automatically finish and the player loses. The player can pick up gems on his way to the sea, each gem has an specific value, when the player pick up a gem, the score will be incremented depending on the gem value.There are also some rocks on the player way, they are not a big deal but, the player can not pass trought them
